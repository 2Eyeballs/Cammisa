About

Cammisa Buerhaus is a theatre artist, writer, and visual artist. For the past 10 years Buerhaus has worked between artistic disciplines, developing a hybrid practice across harsh noise, performance, and visual art. Buerhaus writes and directs confessional-performative psychodramas written in her signature hallucinatory prose.
